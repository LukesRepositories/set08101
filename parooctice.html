Cinnamon Buns

This is a recipe taught to me by the lovely staff of a modern Swedish homestore with a cute foodplace hidden inside it. When I first tried these scrumptious cinnamonny delights I was in awe at the flavour sensation that was ocurring in my mouth. It was like nothing that I had ever tasted before, it hit the spot just when i needed it to, right after wandering round a homestore for an hour. Thats enough waffle from me time to share the recipe.
